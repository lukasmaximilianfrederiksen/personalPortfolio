About me

Hi there! My name is Lukas Maximilian, and I am currently studying multimedia design at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. I am passionate about improving peoples experiences through design and i love working together with motivated creative minds.

Through my education, I've come to love the intersection between design and front-end development. I have studied a variety of subjects centered around Human Centered Design, both physical and digital.
From my studies, my interest grew in Interaction-design and UX-design and how we can design better solutions for people everywhere.
